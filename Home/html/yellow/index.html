About Author

I am a ‘Student’ in Mind, a ‘Teacher’ at Heart, and a ‘Warrior’ in Action.

As a Salespreneur™, I believe that Sales is Social entrepreneurship because every sale is an opportunity to bring in joy and create wealth for the economy.

With professional selling experience in 23 countries and personal selling experience of more than 42 years, I believe that we all are in Sales because we all are selling a product , service or an Idea. In addition to ‘Sales’, ‘Human mind’ , ‘Writing’ and ‘ Evangelizing power of One thing’ are other key areas of interest for me.

Originally from Jhansi, a historic town in Northern India, I currently live in Bangalore suburbs, amidst beautiful farmlands, with my wife Ayesha and two sons Tanish and Aarav.
