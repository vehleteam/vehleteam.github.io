About Book

" Features never Sale, Benefits sometimes Sale,
but UseCase always Sale "

There are 7.7 billion Sales People in the world because everyone in the world is either selling a product, service or an idea. The fact that everyone is in Sales brings up its own challenges and opportunities.
Can one Sales strategy be applied in both Business and Personal context?


How can high growth companies and start-ups successfully win clients against unprecedented competition? Is there a structured and hassle-free way for a Sales Owner to become a Trusted Advisor? Rather than following complex set of processes that don’t work, Is there a simple way to create predictable Sales?


Using more than 20 true stories from Business to Business (B2B), Business to Consumers (B2C) and Personal life, this book gives you one simple and powerful sales strategy that is the perfect answer to all the aforementioned questions. What’s more? You will find a clear blueprint to implement the one strategy very easily


This book is for everyone who wants to create EPIC Sales in Business and in Life.

Why this Book ?

One morning, over breakfast with my wife, a thought occurred to me. What if someone asked me, “If you were to share only one simple thing that has contributed the most to your success in sales, what would that be?”

Interestingly, the answer came to me immediately! This unexpected answer brought a smile to my lips and I decided to write a book because a book offers an amazing opportunity to share and connect with an infinite classroom.

How did the Book Title was choosen ?

Imagine when first draft of the book is ready and book title is not finalised. That was my situation. Title ideas were coming all the time, even during sleep and meditation. Titles like KISS ( Keep it simple and Sell) , ‘The Sales Nirvana’ , ‘From Sales owner to Salespreneur’ were being explored and it was getting difficult to choose.

Finally, I shared 10 titles within my close network. I am glad that current title of the book got maximum votes because it creates aspiration , curiosity and addresses both business and life

Why does book Subtitle has word ‘Life ‘ in it ?

In business context, we are selling products or services. In life, we are all selling ideas.
A child is asking his father to buy an expensive video game
A mother is telling her son not to eat chocolate.
An employee is asking her manager for a salary raise

The book showcases many true stories in personal life and highlights how easily this one sales strategy can be applied to selling ideas in life as well.
